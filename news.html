Flooding in Bangladesh - August 2024

Floods in Bangladesh worsen, killing 18. The flooding is due to tectonic plates uplifting in the Himalayas, increasing sediment erosion. The eroded sediment then clogs up the channel, impeding natural water flow.

Muddy waters are up to chest height, and water floods 11 districts, submerging around 1.5 million people.

The flood was orignially thought to be "displayed inhumanity" by India, where India purposefully opened the dam without warning. However, according to CNN, the flooding was "primarily" the result of water flow in areas of the Gumti River. It was also said to be due to the overflowing of rivers and extreme monsoon rains.



Hasina Flees - August 2024

The Prime Minister of Bangladesh of 15+ years, Sheikh Hasina, finally resigned and later fled after nation-wide movements and dangerous protests occurred. In her replacement, an interim government was formed to govern the country. The movement was led entirely by young students, marking the progression of power to a new younger, determined, and dynamic generation.

Without a proper government, new problems arise. Arms and dangerous weapons are now easily obtainable by anyone, making robberies and crime much more prevalent.

Currently, the ex-PM is hiding out in India.
