Bangladeshi Community of the Greater Seattle Area

Founded in 2015, Projonmo Bangladesh is a Bangladeshi non-profit that aims to connect the Bangladeshi community within the Seattle area. This includes holding cultural events such as festivals, charities, and more.

Projonmo Bangladesh has successfully held many entertainment events, such as Ekushey February events, Winter Festivals, Concerts, and more. Check out "Past Events" to see these events.

Our "Information" tab includes information about local Mosques, Churches, and Temples. Additionally, it includes local Bazaars and grocery stores to get Bengali food, as well as the contact information of local Bengali caterers.

OUR GOAL

  • Strengthen Bangladeshi culture and tradition
  • Foster social engagement and activity
  • Serve as a forum to share information and opinions on topics relevant to its members

GET INVOLVED

  • Check out our News tab to see ongoing events within the community
  • Connect with the Bangladeshi community by joining Facebook groups and getting involved in our social media
  • Contact our members through our "Connect" tab or by joining our Facebook Group, linked on the right side of the navigation bar.
